Week 01: Onboarding Part 1

Between the onboarding meetings and the compliance courses we've learned a ton during our first week at Encora:

On the first day we were shown some of the specifics of the apprentice program, it'll have a duration of about 3 months, the first couple of weeks will be focused on learning about Encora's processes and corporate culture, with the rest being more technical. It is during this latter stage that the hiring and allocation process will start for those who show that their existing skills are already a good match for one of Encora's client companies.

During the introductory week we've had meetings introducing some aspects of Encora: The Mentors, the Leadership Teams, Office Management, Encora People Care, etc.

Mentors are voluntary Encora staff who chose to help develop junior staff, the approach to mentorship at Encora is particular in that it is expected that Mentors put the mentees on the right track to arrive at a conclusion themselves rather than feeding them solutions.

Leadership Teams are groups within the organization, they can vary in their focus, some of the most active on slack are the LGBT team and Cineclub Encora, the people in charge of The Hub are also a LT.

Office Manager oversee the local offices of Encora, they negotiate agreements with local businesses to provide benefits and discounts for those that are part of Encora, they organize events to bring the community together and are also in charge of making sure Encora employees have the right equipment for performing their responsibilities.

Encora People Care is an organization within Encora that looks to get involved with NGOs, some of the events they've organized are blood donation drives, pet food drives, donations for vulnerable groups, Christmas dinners, etc. Participation is completely voluntary, there have been cases where some cause doesn't get enough traction for an event.

The compliance courses were packed with information as well, on Day 1 Orientation Program Encora Overview we learned about some of Encora's corporate leaders and their history. Encora's core values are represented by the anagram ENCODE: Enterprise Value, Curiosity, Ownership, Diversity and Excellence. We also learned about Encora's services, covering a wide range of topics represented by the keywords Envision, Enable, Engineer and Engage.
